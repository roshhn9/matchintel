MatchIntel is a football prediction and match analysis platform focused on probability, performance data, recent form and transparent prediction tracking.

WHO WE ARE

Football predictions backed by analysis

MatchIntel was created to make football match analysis simple, structured and transparent.

Instead of publishing random predictions, every match is presented with useful information such as win probabilities, goal expectations, both-teams-to-score analysis, expected score and prediction confidence.

Our goal is to help football fans understand the reasoning and probability behind a match prediction.

OUR PROCESS

How our match analysis works

01
📊

Collect Match Data

We begin by reviewing relevant information about both teams, including recent results, scoring performance and match context.

02
🔍

Analyse Key Factors

Team form, home and away performance, attacking trends, defensive records and other match factors are considered.

03
📈

Estimate Probabilities

The analysis is converted into percentage probabilities for home win, draw, away win and important goal markets.

04
⚽

Publish Prediction

A main prediction, expected score and confidence percentage are published before the match is completed.

05
✓

Record Final Result

After the match, the actual result is added and the prediction is marked as correct or incorrect.

06
🎯

Update Statistics

Accuracy, recent form, confidence performance and prediction records are recalculated automatically.

MATCH INTELLIGENCE

Football matches can be influenced by many factors. MatchIntel combines different indicators to create a structured match overview.

No single statistic can predict a football result with certainty. The purpose of the analysis is to estimate probabilities based on available information.

⚽
